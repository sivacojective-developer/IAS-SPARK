At IAS SPARK, we are committed to delivering high-quality mentorship and learning experiences. We encourage parents and students to carefully review program details before enrolling. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be considered.

1. Program Enrollment & Fees

Enrollment in IAS SPARK programs is confirmed only after successful payment of the applicable fees. All fees are communicated clearly before registration to ensure full transparency.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered under the following specific conditions:

  • If a cancellation request is made before the commencement of the program.
  • If IAS SPARK cancels the program due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • In exceptional cases reviewed and approved by management.
Refund requests must be submitted in writing to our official support email.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Fees will generally not be refunded in the following cases:

  • Once the program has commenced.
  • For missed sessions due to personal reasons.
  • For partial attendance.
  • For dissatisfaction based on personal expectations after participation.
  • Failure to attend sessions without prior communication.

We strongly encourage parents to attend our counselling or demo sessions before final enrollment.

4. Processing of Approved Refunds

7–14

Working Days for Processing

Original Mode

Refunds are issued back to the original payment source.

Please note that any applicable transaction or administrative charges incurred during the initial payment may be deducted from the final refund amount.

5. Transfer Policy

In certain approved cases, enrollment fees may be transferred to:

  • A future batch of the same program.
  • Another eligible student within the same immediate family.

Such transfers are subject to management review and batch availability.

Submit your refund or transfer request to:

connect.iasspark@gmail.com

6. Policy Modifications: IAS SPARK reserves the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. The latest version will always be available on the website to ensure you are informed of our current terms.
