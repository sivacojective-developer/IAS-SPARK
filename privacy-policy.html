IAS SPARK (“we” “our” “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of students parents and visitors who interact with our website and our learning programs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect use store and safeguard your information when you visit our website register for our programs or communicate with us.

By accessing our website or using our services you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a safe personalised and effective learning experience for every child.

1.1 Personal Information

Collected when you submit a form or contact us:

  • Name of parent and student
  • Email address & Phone number
  • Student’s grade and school
  • Any additional details shared
1.2 Usage Information

Automatically collected during browsing:

  • IP address & Device type
  • Browser info & Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Cookies & tracking data
1.3 Payment Information

If you enroll payment details are collected securely through our payment partners. IAS SPARK does not store card or banking information on its servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries & counseling
  • Enroll students into programs
  • Send updates and schedules
  • Share monthly progress reports
  • Improve website performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain safety & admin records
  • Share policy changes
Note: We do not use children’s data for advertising or any purpose unrelated to learning.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell rent or trade your personal information. We share information only:

  • With mentors and educators involved in our programs
  • With trusted providers (payment gateways communication tools)
  • If required by law or government authorities
  • To protect our rights or prevent misuse

All partners are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We take data protection seriously. Our systems follow industry-standard security measures to safeguard information from unauthorized access misuse loss or alteration.

While we do our best to protect your data no system is fully immune from risks.

5. Children’s Data Protection

Since IAS SPARK is designed for students (Grades 5–10) we ensure:

  • Data is collected only with parental consent
  • No sensitive personal info requested from children
  • Child data is never used for marketing
  • Parents can request deletion at any time

6. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to improve browsing analyze performance and personalize content. You can disable these in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

  • Access/Update your information
  • Request data deletion
  • Withdraw consent anytime

To exercise your rights or ask questions contact us:

connect.iasspark@gmail.com

9. Policy Updates: IAS SPARK may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
